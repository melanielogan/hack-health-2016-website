HackHealth is a one-day, health-themed hackathon hosted by Women in Computer Science. It is devoted to being minority- and beginner-friendly, and aims to provide a comfortable environment for everyone to develop their ideas and improve their skills.

HackHealth may be one day only, but it is a real hackathon! There will be company sponsors, mentors, prizes, food, and, most importantly, company swag!
